## Description
**Freelancer's AI Command Center** is a reference file with 45 structured Claude prompts covering the administrative and business side of freelance work. Each prompt includes a reference ID, a description of its output, and [BRACKETED] placeholders for your specific details.

**Section 1: Client Acquisition (5 prompts)**

Prompts for full project proposals including scope, timeline, and payment terms; scope creep response messages; discovery call question sets; and Statements of Work with numbered deliverables and acceptance criteria.

**Section 3: Client Communication (5 prompts)**
Prompts for difficult client emails, late payment follow-up sequences, project status updates, responses to negative reviews, and contract negotiation replies.

**Section 4: Pricing & Negotiation (3 prompts)**

**Section 5: Operations & Admin (4 prompts)**
Prompts for invoice line item descriptions, project retrospectives, testimonial request messages, and subcontractor briefs with deliverables and acceptance criteria.
